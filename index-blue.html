About US

Karmavesh, is the Official Student Council of Babu Banarasi Das National Institute Of Technology & Management affiliated to AKTU, Lucknow.

We are a group of Students who have a natural inclination to think outside the box, craft and innovate, and raise the ordinary to extraordinary. We believe wholeheartedly in and our guided solely by our motto- " Zeal To Create..."

We are led by a desire to create things, build things, and do the things other people seldom have the guts to do. We're unfazed by challenges and obstacles. Once we set our minds to something, it is all we see until our vision has reached completion.

We don't rest, we don't pause. We soldier it on, through it all. There's nothing we fear, nothing we worry about because we are a team and together we tackle the greatest hurdles and follow a path that has been created by no one, but ourselves.
